Color Scheme

The website colors are inspired in the characteristics of Sonora state, a desertic ecosytem near to the sea and the logo is a representation of the Seris, an indigenous community who capture geometric figures in the art of the baskets that they manufacture to bring economic sustenance to their community. Also there's black and white to create contrast.
